Oldest Township

Langa Cultural Tour

Langa gives a true glimpse into the history and heart of Cape Town. About 15 km from the city centre, it is the oldest township in Cape Town, celebrating its centenary this year.

  • ✓ Dompas Museum: Steeped in apartheid history
  • ✓ Traditional Beer: Taste Umqombothi sorghum beer
  • ✓ Local Delicacies: Experience "Smiley" sheep heads
  • ✓ Guga Sthebe: Arts, theatre, and ceramic murals
  • ✓ Informal Settlement: A glimpse into Langa's heart
  • ✓ Drums & Marimbas: Traditional musical welcome
⏱ 90 Minutes 💰 From R500/person
