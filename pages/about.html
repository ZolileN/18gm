About 18 Township Tours

Who We Are

Authentic Township Stories

We are an award-winning tour operator that provides authentic township tour experiences. Ours is to provide our guests with an experience that only a local can give. We share authentic township stories and realities. We are locals, our guides are local and our stories are authentic.

Our journey began in 2011, when we saw the need to provide the global community with a unique opportunity to experience our beautiful and diverse community. We saw how tourism can change lives and how it can be used as an agent of change. A challenge with Cape Town is that people often visit but never get to truly experience the people; we saw this as the perfect opportunity to connect the world with our people from the Cape.

We use tourism as a tool to connect people, change lives and positively contribute to societal change. 18 Township Tours demystifies the township and makes it accessible to the world. Safety is our number one priority, and making our clients feel at home is what we do best. Book with us and learn, have fun and far more importantly contribute to community change.
